# Medibank AI-Powered Conversion Operating System

## Overview

The Medibank Admin Engine is no longer positioned as a traditional CRM, analytics dashboard, or campaign management platform.

Instead, the platform is evolving into an:

# AI-powered operational intelligence and student lifecycle orchestration system

The core purpose of the system is to help Medibank:

* increase OSHC to OVHC conversion
* improve engagement across the student lifecycle
* reduce churn
* improve app adoption
* operationalise referral-led growth
* identify high-value opportunities
* support students throughout their transition journey

The platform achieves this through a Human-in-the-Loop AI model where specialised AI agents continuously analyse behavioural, engagement, referral, campaign, and lifecycle signals to surface opportunities, risks, and recommendations.

IMPORTANT:
The AI agents should NOT be represented as literal robots/chatbots across the UI.

This is a prototype.

Instead, the UI should subtly communicate:

* AI-generated insights
* AI-recommended actions
* confidence scores
* estimated business impact
* operational intelligence
* recommendation reasoning

The experience should feel:

* intelligent
* proactive
* operational
* premium
* enterprise-grade
* highly strategic

NOT:

* fully autonomous
* robotic
* overwhelming
* generic analytics dashboard

The system should feel like:

* an AI-assisted operational command centre
* a modern enterprise intelligence platform
* Apple + Stripe + Linear inspired enterprise UX
* premium dark-mode ecosystem with layered intelligence

---

# Human-in-the-Loop Governance Model

The platform follows a Human-in-the-Loop (HITL) operational model.

This means:

* AI continuously analyses data and surfaces recommendations
* Humans review, approve, and execute actions

AI agents DO NOT autonomously launch campaigns or contact students.

Instead, the platform should show:

* recommended actions
* why recommendations were generated
* estimated revenue or engagement impact
* confidence scores
* suggested timing and channels

All operational decisions remain under human control.

# AI Agent Ecosystem

Again:
DO NOT visualise these agents literally.

Their outputs should appear naturally throughout the interface.

↓

↓
